Vegetable Sandwich

This simple vegetable sandwich is quick to prepare, healthy, and perfect for breakfast or a light snack.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Spread butter evenly on the bread slices.
  2. Place tomato and cucumber slices on one slice of bread.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover with another bread slice and serve.
